Henry TN

Henry TN, whose real name is **Henry Tran** (Vietnamese: Trần Quốc Cường), is a Vietnamese-born artist now living in Auckland, New Zealand. He was born on **20 May 2010** in Đồng Hới city, Vietnam, and after spending about twelve years in Vietnam he moved with his family to New Zealand, where he attended Northcote College. Under the stage name Henry TN, he released his first EP *TN love Instrumental 1* and later projects such as *Fellahshy*. In much of his music, he takes roles as musician.

Actor, Creative

Yuna Tran

**Yuna Tran** (Trần Ngọc Bảo Châu) is a Vietnamese-born actor now living in Auckland, New Zealand. She was born on **18 July 2012** in Đồng Hới city, Vietnam. After spending 10 years in Vietnam, she moved with her family to New Zealand. Yuna Tran is **Henry TN's sister**. In all Music Video projects, she always takes a role as the main actor in the music video, lending her creative presence to the visual side of the music.
